At Upstream Technical Services, we offer a comprehensive range of technical services designed to meet the unique needs of the oil and gas industry, power generation plants, and various other industrial sectors. Our commitment to efficiency, reliability, and safety ensures we deliver exceptional service and innovative solutions.

Service Highlights

Oil and Gas Field Services

Our specialized teams offer comprehensive onshore and offshore support, delivering installation, maintenance, and repair services tailored to enhance your upstream operations' efficiency and reliability.

Maintenance Services

Count on our skilled technicians for expert maintenance and repairs on pumps, generators, and valves. We ensure optimal performance and extend the longevity of your equipment with meticulous care and attention.

Retail and Wholesale

Explore our high-quality electrical generators and accessories for retail purchase.Additionally, Partner with us for wholesale prefabricated houses and parts, offering durable and cost-effective housing solutions.

Equipment Rental

Optimize your operations with our diverse rental equipment solutions:
