Upstream Technical Services, established in Abu Dhabi, UAE, in 2016, is a leading provider of customised technical solutions for the oil and gas, power generation, and industrial sectors. we have been providing innovative and reliable solutions in the oil and gas industry, with expertise in Engineering, Procurement, Installation, and Commissioning (EPIC) services. With a foundation built on over 30 years of combined industry experience in the Middle East and Asia, our company is dedicated to exceeding client expectations through innovation, reliability, and a steadfast commitment to safety. Our technical know-how in EPIC services, maintenance, project management, and more, ensures superior quality and efficiency in every endeavour
